Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get notified when a book I want becomes available?

Yes! Add the book to your Wishlist. You’ll receive a notification when it’s listed by another user.

How do I list a book?

Go to your Library and click “Add Book.” You can choose whether to sell, swap, or make the book available for offers. Set the condition and price if applicable.

How does swapping work?

When you find a book you want, you can offer one of your listed books in exchange. The owner can accept, decline, or counter-offer, up to 3 back-and-forth rounds are allowed.

How is shipping handled?

Shipping is flat-rate. Once an offer is accepted, you can generate a label from your Profile page.

What if a book is damaged or not as described?

Users are required to specify the condition of books accurately. You can leave a rating and report any issues in the Profile or Notifications section.
