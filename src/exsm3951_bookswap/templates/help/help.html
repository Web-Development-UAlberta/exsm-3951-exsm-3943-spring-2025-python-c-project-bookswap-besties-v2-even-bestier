Go to your Library and click “Add Book.” You can choose whether to sell, swap, or make the book available for offers. Set the condition and price if applicable.
When you find a book you want, you can offer one of your listed books in exchange. The owner can accept, decline, or counter-offer, with up to 3 back-and-forth rounds.
Shipping is flat-rate and simple! Once your swap or purchase is confirmed, you can generate a shipping label directly from your Profile & Settings page. Just enter the recipient’s address, package weight, and choose either Standard or Priority shipping. A printable label will be created for you, complete with tracking. You’ll also get a confirmation number to track your shipment.
